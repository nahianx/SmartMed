# For Patients

## Getting Started

### System Requirements

SmartMed works on:
- **Desktop Browsers:** Chrome 90+, Firefox 90+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- **Mobile Browsers:** Chrome (Android), Safari (iOS 14+)
- **Internet Connection:** Broadband or mobile data recommended

### Accessing SmartMed

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to [SmartMed URL]
3. Sign in or create a new account

---

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Registration

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the homepage
2. Choose **"Patient"** as your role
3. Enter your information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Date of birth
   - Password (must be at least 8 characters)

### Step 2: Email Verification

1. Check your email inbox for a verification link
2. Click the link to verify your email
3. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

After verification:
1. Add your medical information (optional but recommended)
2. Set up two-factor authentication (recommended)
3. Review privacy settings

### Password Requirements

Your password must include:
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter
- One lowercase letter
- One number
- One special character (!@#$%^&*)

---

## Managing Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click your name or avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **"Profile"** from the dropdown menu

### Editing Personal Information

1. Go to **Profile** → **Edit Profile**
2. Update your information:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Emergency contact
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Medical Information

Keep your medical information up to date for better care:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Medical Information**
2. Add or update:
   - **Allergies:** Drug allergies, food allergies
   - **Medical History:** Previous conditions, surgeries
   - **Current Medications:** List all medications you take
3. Click **"Save"**

> **Note:** This information is confidential and only shared with your healthcare providers.

### Uploading a Profile Photo

1. Go to **Profile** → **Edit Profile**
2. Click on the avatar image
3. Select a photo from your device
4. Adjust the crop if needed
5. Click **"Upload"**

## Finding a Doctor

### Browsing Doctors

1. Click **"Find a Doctor"** in the main navigation
2. Browse the list of available doctors

### Filtering Doctors

Use filters to narrow your search:
- **Specialty:** Cardiology, Dermatology, General Practice, etc.
- **Availability:** Available today, this week
- **Rating:** 4+ stars, 5 stars
- **Location:** If applicable

### Viewing Doctor Profiles

Click on a doctor's name to see:
- Professional background
- Specializations
- Available appointment times
- Patient reviews (if available)
- Consultation fees

### Searching for Doctors

1. Use the search bar at the top
2. Enter:
   - Doctor's name
   - Specialty
   - Condition or symptom
3. Press Enter or click Search

---

## Booking Appointments

### Step 1: Select a Doctor

1. Find a doctor using search or browse
2. Click **"Book Appointment"** on their profile

### Step 2: Choose Date and Time

1. Select an available date from the calendar
2. Choose a time slot (available slots shown in green)
3. View the appointment duration

### Step 3: Provide Details

1. Select appointment type:
   - New consultation
   - Follow-up
   - Check-up
2. Add notes for the doctor (symptoms, concerns)
3. Review the consultation fee

### Step 4: Confirm Booking

1. Review all appointment details
2. Confirm your contact information
3. Click **"Confirm Booking"**
4. You'll receive a confirmation email

### Booking Tips

- Book in advance for popular doctors
- Keep your phone number updated for reminders
- Add notes about your symptoms for better preparation

## Managing Appointments

### Viewing Your Appointments

1. Go to **"My Appointments"** in the navigation
2. View appointments by status:
   - **Upcoming:** Scheduled appointments
   - **Past:** Completed appointments
   - **Cancelled:** Cancelled appointments

### Appointment Details

Click on any appointment to see:
- Date and time
- Doctor information
- Appointment notes
- Status

### Rescheduling an Appointment

1. Go to **My Appointments**
2. Click on the appointment you want to reschedule
3. Click **"Reschedule"**
4. Select a new date and time
5. Confirm the change

> **Note:** Some appointments may have rescheduling restrictions. Check with your doctor's office.

### Cancelling an Appointment

1. Go to **My Appointments**
2. Click on the appointment
3. Click **"Cancel Appointment"**
4. Provide a reason (optional)
5. Confirm cancellation

> **Important:** Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance when possible.

## Using the Queue System

SmartMed's virtual queue system helps manage your waiting time.

### Joining the Queue

1. On the day of your appointment, you'll be added to the queue
2. Go to **"Queue Status"** to check your position

### Understanding Your Queue Position

- **Position:** Your place in line (e.g., #3)
- **Estimated Wait:** Approximate time until your turn
- **Status:** Waiting, In Progress, or Completed

### Real-Time Updates

The queue updates automatically:
- Watch your position change in real-time
- Receive notifications when you're next
- Get alerts if there are delays

### Tips for the Queue

- Stay logged in to receive updates
- Don't leave if you're in the top 3 positions
- Check notifications for your turn

## Viewing Prescriptions

### Accessing Your Prescriptions

1. Go to **"Prescriptions"** in the main navigation
2. View all your prescriptions

### Prescription Details

Each prescription shows:
- **Medication name**
- **Dosage:** Amount per dose
- **Frequency:** How often to take
- **Duration:** How long to take
- **Instructions:** Special instructions
- **Prescribing doctor**
- **Date prescribed**

### Downloading Prescriptions

1. Click on a prescription
2. Click **"Download PDF"**
3. The prescription will download to your device

### Sharing Prescriptions

To share with a pharmacy:
1. Click on the prescription
2. Click **"Share"**
3. A secure link will be generated
4. Copy the link or send directly

> **Note:** Share links expire after 24 hours for security.

### Prescription Refills

If you need a refill:
1. Go to the prescription
2. Click **"Request Refill"**
3. Your doctor will be notified
4. Check back for approval status

## Account Security

### Setting Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Strongly recommended for protecting your medical information:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Open your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Scan the QR code
5. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
6. Save your backup codes in a safe place

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **"Update Password"**

### Password Reset

If you forgot your password:
1. Click **"Forgot Password"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Click the link and create a new password

### Viewing Login History

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Scroll to **"Login History"**
3. Review recent login activity
4. If you see suspicious activity, change your password immediately

## Managing Your Schedule

### Setting Availability

1. Go to **"Schedule"** in the navigation
2. Click **"Set Availability"**
3. Select days and times you're available
4. Set appointment duration (e.g., 30 minutes)
5. Save your schedule

### Blocking Time Off

1. Go to **Schedule** → **Block Time**
2. Select the date(s) to block
3. Add a reason (optional)
4. Existing appointments will be notified

### Viewing Your Calendar

1. Go to **"Schedule"**
2. View by Day, Week, or Month
3. Click appointments for details
4. Color codes indicate status:
   - Green: Confirmed
   - Yellow: Pending
   - Red: Cancelled
   - Gray: Past

## Viewing Patient Information

### Accessing Patient Records

1. Click on a patient's name from:
   - Appointments list
   - Queue view
   - Search results
2. View their profile

### Patient Profile Sections

- **Personal Information:** Contact details
- **Medical History:** Past conditions, surgeries
- **Allergies:** Drug and food allergies
- **Current Medications:** Active prescriptions
- **Previous Visits:** Past appointment notes
- **Prescriptions:** Prescription history

### Adding Notes

During or after an appointment:

5. Save the note

> **Important:** All patient data is confidential and protected under HIPAA.

## Creating Prescriptions

### New Prescription

1. Open the patient's profile
2. Click **"New Prescription"**
3. Fill in prescription details:
   - **Medication:** Search or enter drug name
   - **Dosage:** Amount per dose
   - **Frequency:** Times per day
   - **Duration:** Treatment length
   - **Instructions:** Special instructions
   - **Refills:** Number of refills allowed
4. Review for drug interactions
5. Click **"Create Prescription"**

### Drug Interaction Check

SmartMed automatically checks for:
- Interactions with current medications
- Allergy conflicts
- Dosage recommendations

If interactions are found:
1. Review the warning
2. Adjust the prescription if needed
3. Acknowledge if proceeding

### Prescription History

1. Go to **"Prescriptions"** in the navigation
2. Filter by:
   - Patient
   - Date range
   - Medication
3. Click any prescription for full details

# General

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Can't log in?**
1. Verify your email address is correct
2. Try resetting your password
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issues persist

**2FA not working?**
1. Ensure your device time is correct
2. Try using backup codes
3. Contact support for account recovery

### Loading Problems

**Page won't load?**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try incognito/private mode
5. Try a different browser

### Notification Issues

**Not receiving notifications?**
1. Check notification settings are enabled
2. Check email spam folder
3. Verify phone number for SMS
4. Allow browser notifications

### Appointment Issues

**Can't book appointment?**
- Doctor may be fully booked
- Check if you have conflicts
- Try a different date
- Contact the clinic directly

## Privacy & Security

### Your Data Protection

SmartMed is committed to protecting your medical information:

- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Control:** Only authorized personnel can view your data
- **Audit Logging:** All access to your records is logged
- **HIPAA Compliance:** We follow healthcare privacy regulations

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your medical records
- Request corrections to your information
- Know who has accessed your records
- Request data deletion (where applicable)

### Reporting Security Concerns

If you notice suspicious activity:
1. Change your password immediately
2. Contact support@smartmed.com
3. Review your login history
